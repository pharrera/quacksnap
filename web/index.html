Why QuackSnap

Everything you'd want from AirDrop — for Windows and iPhone.

Instant on your network

On the same Wi-Fi, transfers go straight over an encrypted local connection. Half-a-second fast, nothing leaves your network.

Both directions

Snip on your PC, or tap to send photos and files back from your phone. Same speed, same encryption — it just works both ways.

Any file — even text

PDFs, video, zips, documents, anything. Highlighted text you copy lands on your phone's clipboard, ready to paste.

Arrives even when locked

Off your network? QuackSnap sends an encrypted blob through a relay and a push — the screenshot lands on your lock screen without opening the app.

Pair with a 6-digit code

Type the code your PC shows — your phone finds it automatically. No sign-in, no QR hunting. QR and links work too.

End-to-end encrypted

Files are sealed to your device's key. The relay and Apple only ever see ciphertext — nobody in the middle can read what you send.

Private by design

Only your two devices can read a thing.

Every transfer is encrypted end-to-end between your PC and your phone. Devices trust each other by a certificate pinned when you pair — so even on a hostile network, no one can impersonate your computer or peek at what you send.

When a file has to travel through the relay, it's already sealed to your phone's key before it leaves your PC. The relay stores nothing it can open, and forgets it within minutes.

Questions

Good to know.

Is it really free?

Yes — the Windows app and the iPhone app are both free. No account, no subscription.

Does it need the internet?

No. On the same Wi-Fi, transfers go directly between your two devices and never leave your network. When your phone is away, an encrypted relay + push delivers it — but the contents stay sealed to your phone.

Can I send from my iPhone to my PC too?

Yes. Tap the send button in the app to push photos or any files back to your computer, over the same encrypted connection. They land in a QuackSnap folder on your PC.

What can I send?

Screenshots, photos, video, PDFs, documents, zips — any file. Copied text can be sent too, and lands on your phone's clipboard ready to paste.

How secure is it?

Every transfer is end-to-end encrypted and authenticated. Your devices pin each other's certificates when you pair, so nothing in the middle — not even the relay or Apple — can read what you send.
